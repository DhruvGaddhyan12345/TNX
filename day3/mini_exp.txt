What is Data-Centric AI?

Data-Centric AI focuses on systematically improving the quality of data instead of continuously increasing model complexity. The approach emphasizes better labels, cleaner datasets, and consistent annotations. High-quality data often produces larger improvements than larger models.

Why do Top Kaggle Competitors Spend Significant Time on EDA?

EDA helps competitors understand data distributions, identify hidden patterns, detect leakage, and uncover quality issues. These insights guide feature engineering and validation strategies. As a result, EDA often provides a competitive advantage.

What is Data Leakage and Why is it Dangerous?

Data leakage occurs when information unavailable during real-world prediction is accidentally included during training. This leads to unrealistically high validation performance. Models affected by leakage usually perform poorly when deployed on truly unseen data.
